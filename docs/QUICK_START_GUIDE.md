# Breeder Management App - Quick Start Guide

## Welcome to Your Breeder Management System!

This guide will help you get started with the Breeder Management App in just 15 minutes.

---

## Step 1: Account Setup (3 minutes)

### Create Your Account

1. Visit the app login page
2. Click **"Sign Up"**
3. Enter your email and create a strong password
4. Click **"Create Account"**
5. Check your email for verification link
6. Click the verification link to activate your account

### Log In

1. Return to the login page
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **"Sign In"**

**‚úì You're now logged in!**

---

## Step 2: Set Up Your Breeder Profile (4 minutes)

### Access Settings

1. Click **"Settings"** in the sidebar (bottom left)
2. You'll see the Breeder Settings page

### Fill In Your Information

Complete these essential fields:

**Kennel Information**

- Kennel Name: _Your kennel/business name_
- Description: _Brief description of your breeding program_
- Breed: _Primary breed(s) you work with_

**Location**

- City: _Your city_
- State: _Your state_

**Contact Information**

- Phone: _Your phone number_
- Email: _Your contact email_
- Website: _Your website URL (if you have one)_

**Social Media** (Optional)

- Facebook, Instagram, Twitter links

### Upload Your Logo

1. Click **"Upload Logo"** button
2. Select your kennel logo image
3. Crop if needed
4. Click **"Save"**

### Save Your Profile

1. Scroll to bottom
2. Click **"Save Profile"**

**‚úì Your profile is complete!**

---

## Step 3: Add Your First Dog (4 minutes)

### Open the Add Dog Dialog

1. Click **"Dogs"** in the sidebar
2. Click the **"+ Add Dog"** button (top right)

### Enter Basic Information

**Required Fields:**

- **Name**: Enter the dog's name
- **Breed**: Start typing and select from dropdown (280+ breeds available)
- **Sex**: Select Male or Female
- **Date of Birth**: Click to select date

**Recommended Fields:**

- **Color**: Dog's color
- **Markings**: Any distinctive markings
- **Weight**: Current weight (you can update this anytime)

### Add a Photo

1. Click **"Upload Photo"** or drag-and-drop
2. Select photo from your computer
3. Crop if desired
4. Click **"Save"**

### Set Program Status

Choose the appropriate status:

- **Breeding Dog**: Active in your breeding program
- **Guardian Home**: Lives with a guardian family
- **External Stud**: Not owned by you, used for breeding
- **Retired**: No longer breeding

### Add Registration Info (Optional)

- **Registration Number**: AKC/CKC number
- **Registration Status**: Registered, Pending, or Not Registered

### Save Your Dog

1. Review all information
2. Click **"Add Dog"** button at bottom

**‚úì Your first dog is added!**

---

## Step 4: Explore Your Dashboard (2 minutes)

### View Your Dashboard

1. Click **"Dashboard"** in sidebar (home icon at top)

### What You'll See

**Statistics Cards**

- Total Dogs count
- Active Dams (breeding females)
- Active Sires (breeding males)
- Puppies Available
- This Year's Income

**Upcoming Heats** (for female dogs)

- System predicts next heat cycles
- Based on previous heat cycle data

**Recent Activity**

- Quick overview of recent changes
- New inquiries
- Upcoming events

### Take a Quick Tour

- Click on different statistics to see details
- Explore the various sections
- Get familiar with the layout

**‚úì You know where everything is!**
